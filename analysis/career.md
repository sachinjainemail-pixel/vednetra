## Diagnostic factors (ranked by measured signal)

1. **Lagna-lord afflicted + dusthana clustering / loaded 7th (female) → not-working.**
   Strongest, cleanest factor. Lagna lord combust / debilitated / at ~0° / in the 6-8-12, combined with
   a cluster of debilitated-combust planets in a dusthana, OR (for women) a 7th house tenanted by 2+
   planets incl. a malefic. Fires on 764, 796, 813, 814, 824, 830, 752, 753, 755, 758 = **10/12 not-working
   caught**; only misses 801 (unemployed but Shasha-yoga chart) and 827 (studying, Sun exalted). Precision high.
   Female base-rate itself is only ~44% homemaker (7/16), so gender alone is a coin-flip — the *affliction*
   is what discriminates.

2. **Sun in the 10th, or Sun+Saturn conjunct in a kendra → government.** 773 (Sun+Sat+Me+Ve in H4 kendra,
   both government karakas → gov job), 823 (Sun in H10 → Army Colonel). Fired 2×, hit 2×. Small n but
   classically exact; Sun/Saturn on the career axis = state/uniformed authority. Weakness: it does NOT catch
   the *semi/contract* gov cases 777 (Saturn=LL in 6th) and 798 (10th lord Venus in 5th with Sun/Sat/Ju),
   which read as generic "service."

3. **Strong 6th lord (in 6th, own/exalted) or Saturn ruling the career axis → service (job).** 803 is the
   textbook case: 6th lord Jupiter in 6th own → private job (poster's own reason). 777: Saturn(=LL) in 6th →
   semi-gov service. 802/774: Saturn as 6th/7th lord tying lagna to career → job. The 6th house of paid
   service is a genuine service marker in the data.

4. **Mars in own/exalted in a kendra → business — but only ~60% reliable.** Hits: 767 (Mars own H4), 800
   (Mars exa H7), 822 (Mars own H4), 754 (Mars own H1, contractor), 781 (Mars exa H1). **Misses: 816** (Mars
   own H4 but she took a *private job*), **792** (Mars own H1 → private job), **794** (Mars own *in the 10th*
   → school *teacher*, service). So Mars-in-own-kendra raises the business odds but is not decisive — pair it
   with a business-leaning 7th/3rd or absence of a strong 6th lord.

5. **7th lord placed in / aspecting the 10th, or 7th lord in own-exalted well-placed → business/self-employment.**
   768 (7th lord Jupiter aspects own 7th → business), 782 (7th lord Mars *in the 10th* → tea business).
   BUT this over-fires: 762 (7th lord Venus own but in 12th → still private job — dusthana kills it), 757
   (7th lord Jupiter own in H4 → still a private IT job), 801 (7th lord Saturn own in 7th, Shasha yoga →
   *unemployed*). Rule of thumb that emerged: 7th-lord dignity only means business when the 7th lord is
   **out of the dusthanas** and the 6th lord is not simultaneously strong.

6. **Leo lagna (lagna-lord Sun) + weak/debilitated 7th lord → self-employed / own work (teaching, consulting).**
   763: poster's own logic — "Sun lagnesh → ego → can't hold a job; debilitated 7th lord Saturn → teaches
   tuitions." Matches the classical Sun-ego-to-self-employment idea. n=1 clean, plus consistent with the
   many Leo-lagna businessmen (767, 815, 822).

7. **Rahu → foreign posting, NOT business.** In this data Rahu tracks *going abroad for a job* more than
   entrepreneurship: 819 (Rahu H7 → job in America), 802 (Rahu H1 → engineer abroad), 795 (Rahu H12 →
   foreign-linked CEO), 820 (labour in Dubai). Do not read Rahu as a business karaka here.

8. **D10 (Dashamsha) — untestable from this data.** The topic files contain only the D1 (rasi) chart. D10
   is cited in four expert REASON lines (804 "Saturn own in D10 H2", 808 "Saturn's D10 position", 793, 795)
   but no D10 chart is provided, so its predictive value cannot be measured here — flagged as a gap, not a
   result.

## Decision procedure (apply in order; stop at first match)

1. **GOVERNMENT** if Sun is in the 10th house, OR Sun is conjunct Saturn in a kendra (1/4/7/10), OR the
   10th lord sits with both Sun and Saturn.
2. **NOT-WORKING / HOMEMAKER** if the lagna lord is combust / debilitated / at very low degree / in the
   6-8-12, AND either (a) 2+ planets are debilitated-or-combust clustered in a dusthana, or (b) [female]
   the 7th house holds 2+ planets including a malefic.
3. **OWN-BUSINESS** if Mars is in own/exalted sign in a kendra, OR the 7th lord is in the 10th house, OR the
   7th lord is in own/exalted sign outside the dusthanas (or aspects the 7th) — provided the 6th lord is not
   itself strong-in-6th.
4. **SELF-EMPLOYED-PROFESSIONAL** if lagna is Leo (LL Sun) with a weak/debilitated 7th lord (ego + no
   partnership/service → own practice, teaching, consulting).
5. **PRIVATE-JOB (default/service)** otherwise — reinforced by a strong 6th lord, or Saturn ruling/aspecting
   the lagna-10th axis.

## Caveats
- **Business vs service is only ~73% separable** here; classical 7th-lord and Mars rules over-predict business.
  When a strong 6th lord or Saturn also touches the career axis, service wins even with a dignified 7th lord.
- **Government sub-types (permanent vs semi/contract) are not separable** from D1 alone — 777 and 798 read as
  generic service.
- **D10 could not be tested** (no D10 charts supplied); experts leaned on it for 4 charts, so real-world
  accuracy of a D10-inclusive method is likely higher than the D1-only 71% measured here.
- **Small buckets:** government (4) and self-employed-professional (2) are too thin for firm hit-rates — treat
  rules 2, 5, 6 as directional.
- **Chart-vs-reality gaps exist:** 801 has a full Shasha-mahapurusha business yoga yet is unemployed; 827 has
  an exalted Sun yet is a years-long exam aspirant — timing (dasha) and free will override the natal signature.
