**Core test:** does the Maha/Antar/Pratyantar lord operative at the event equal the **significator of the relevant house** — where "significator" = the house *lord*, an *occupant* of that house, its *karaka*, or a planet *aspecting* it?

### Diagnostic factors (ranked)
1. **Dasha lord = LORD of the relevant house.** Cleanest fits. [794] Saturn-MD is the 7th lord *and sits in the 7th (own)* → marriage. [801] Saturn-MD is the 8th lord → operation. [824] Mars-MD/AD is the 4th lord → moved to maternal home. [754] Mars-MD is the 6th lord (separation) → divorce. [758] Jupiter-AD is the 4th lord → house sale.
2. **Dasha lord = OCCUPANT of the relevant house.** [793a] Rahu-MD occupies the 5th (romance) → love breakup. [820a] Ketu (in the 5th) active → daughter born. [820b] Moon exalted in the 7th active in the sub-period → marriage. [790] Rahu-AD in the 8th (mortality) → spouse's death. [829] Venus-MD in the 12th (confinement) → jail.
3. **Dasha lord = KARAKA of the event.** [772] Venus (marriage karaka), exalted, active in the operative sub-string → marriage. [824] Moon (mother karaka) in the pratyantar → maternal-home move.
4. **Dasha lord ASPECTS the significator.** [765] Jupiter-MD aspects the 5th lord Venus → education (MBA).
5. **Dusthana-lord routing for the flavour of the event.** Sale/loss → 12th significator ([758] Ketu-MD in 12th); separation/dissolution → 6th/8th lords and Rahu-Saturn ([754],[793]).

### Decision / verification procedure
To time (or confirm) an event on a chart:
1. Identify the **relevant house(s)** for the event (marriage→7; children→5; property→4, sale adds 12; health/surgery→6/8; death of spouse→8 & 2-from-7; education→5/9; imprisonment/exit-from-home→12; return-to-mother's-home→4 + Moon).
2. List that house's **significators**: its lord, any occupants, its karaka, and any aspecting planet.
3. Find the **Maha/Antar/Pratyantar** running at the candidate date (from MahaSeq + dates; posters' REASON lines usually name the exact string).
4. The event is **expected/confirmed** when at least one of the running dasha lords is in that significator set. Prefer periods where the **lord OR an occupant** of the house runs; a karaka- or aspect-only link is a weaker (but still valid) confirmation.

### Highest-signal rules
1. **Marriage fires when the 7th lord, a 7th occupant, or Venus runs.** 3/3 — [794] Saturn(7th lord in 7th), [820b] Moon-exa-in-7th, [772] Venus-karaka.
2. **Relationship rupture fires under Rahu/Saturn tied to the 5th/7th.** [793a/b/c] all in Rahu-MD (Rahu in the 5th) with Saturn/Jupiter (5th/7th lord) sub-periods; [754] Mars = 6th lord.
3. **Surgery/health fires in the 6th/8th-lord dasha.** [801] Saturn = 8th lord MD.
4. **Property/relocation fires in the 4th-lord (or 12th for sale) dasha.** [758] Jupiter-4th-lord AD + Ketu-12th MD; [824] Mars-4th-lord MD.
5. **Children fire in the dasha of a 5th-house occupant or the 5th lord.** [820a] Ketu-in-5th.
6. **"Significator" must be read broadly** — lord OR occupant OR karaka OR aspector. Under the narrow "sign-lord only" reading the hit-rate drops to ~6/15; the occupant/karaka extension is what lifts it to 13/15.

### Caveats
- **Exact event dates are missing for several entries** ([754],[761],[790 is 2021],[829 is 2019]); where the date is old, the operative Maha was inferred from MahaSeq + `Maha@2026`, so those verdicts ([790],[829]) are plausibility judgments, not verified.
- **Multi-level confirmation.** Posters routinely cite 4–5 dasha levels (MD/AD/PD/sookshma) plus **transit (gochar)** and **navamsa** ([772],[794],[801],[793c]). The single-level "MD/AD lord = house lord" test understates their actual method; [793c] only resolves in D9.
- **Direction, not magnitude.** The rule tells *whether the theme is active*, not the event's polarity (marriage vs. spouse-leaving both fire under 7th-linked dashas); malefic vs. benefic sub-lords and house-of-affliction routing are needed to fix the sign of the outcome.
- **Wealth-timing is the known gap** — natal wealth promise did not predict the *year* via an 11th/2nd-lord dasha in the one test case ([761]).
