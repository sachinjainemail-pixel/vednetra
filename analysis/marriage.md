### Diagnostic factors (ranked)

**1. Manglik-Y as a divorce/separation multiplier (strong, conditional).**
Among the 9 divorced/separated, **7 are Manglik-Y** (754,792,796,798,800,815,819); only 773,788 are N. Among the 16 stable-married, only 6 are Manglik-Y. So conditional on *being married*, Manglik roughly doubles separation odds (7/13 married-Manglik ended vs 2/... ). Manglik does NOT predict unmarried (8 Y / 7 N among unmarried ≈ base rate) and does NOT by itself prevent a happy marriage (764,824,830 are Manglik-Y and stable). Signal = **Manglik + a malefic actually loading the 7th axis with no benefic there.**

**2. Malefic on the 7th house *without benefic support* → separation/divorce (strong).**
Mars/Rahu/Saturn sitting in or hard-aspecting the 7th, and no Jup/Ven/waxing-Moon in the 7th:
- 796 Saturn(exa) in 7th → divorce in 1 yr; 800 Mars(exa)+Rahu in 7th → wife left; 815 Mars(R) in 7th → dual divorce; 819 Rahu in 7th → 1st marriage divorced in 2 months.
Contrast the *protected* Manglik charts where a benefic co-tenants the 7th and the marriage held: **764** (Jup+Ven in 7th), **824** (Moon own in 7th), **830** (Venus own in 7th). Benefic in the 7th is the single best "cancels the damage" marker.

**3. 7th-lord weak & unsupported → unmarried (moderate).**
Unmarried natives repeatedly have the **7th lord in a dusthana (6/8/12), debilitated, or combust, with no benefic aspect on the 7th** — and their own REASON lines say exactly this:
- 761 (7L Mars in 8th + Ketu), 763 (7L Saturn deb in 9th, "no benefic aspect on 7th"), 765 (7L Jupiter R in 12th enemy), 767 (7L Saturn R in 8th), 811 (7L Moon in 8th), 825 (7L Jupiter comb in 12th), 752 (7L Venus with Ketu, "no benefic aspect on 7th; Rahu aspects 7L").
Weakness of the 7th lord is necessary-ish but not sufficient (several married charts also have a dusthana 7th-lord, e.g. 774 Moon in 8th, 811-type) — it must combine with an afflicted karaka and no benefic on the 7th.

**4. Marriage karaka afflicted (Venus♂ / Jupiter♀) → unmarried or widowed (moderate).**
- Karaka **conjunct Ketu** or **in the 12th while weak** shows up in the singles/widowers: 752 (Jupiter+Ketu), 761 ("karaka Venus… afflicted"), 781/822 (Venus deb / combust-retro), 829 (Venus in 12th → relationship not marriage), and the two catchable widowers **808 & 826 (Venus in 12th)**. Karaka in **own/exalted** 12th does NOT deny (762 Venus own in 12th = married) — dignity matters.

**5. 5th-house strength & 5th↔7th linkage → LOVE vs arranged (clean on small n).** — see split below.

**6. Widowhood is largely NOT readable** from these six factors. Only 2/5 widowers (808,826) fit (karaka in 12th). 771,790,802 have Venus *exalted/strong* — widowhood there is a spouse-longevity (8th-from-7th / 2nd-house maraka) matter, not a 7th-house affliction. Treat as a residual.

### Decision procedure (apply in order)

1. **Widow screen (weak).** If marriage karaka (♂Venus/♀Jupiter) is in the 12th *and weak* (not own/exalted) or tightly conjunct Ketu, AND the 8th/2nd axis is malefic-loaded → flag **widowed**. (Expect to catch only ~40% of widowers; otherwise it falls through.)
2. **Unmarried screen.** If 7th lord is in a dusthana (6/8/12) OR debilitated OR combust, **AND** no benefic (Jup/Ven/Mer/waxing Moon) sits in or aspects the 7th, **AND** the karaka is also afflicted (with Ketu, in 12th weak, debilitated, or combust) → predict **unmarried / no-marriage (live-in or affair)**.
3. **Divorce/separation screen.** If **Manglik-Y** AND (a malefic — Mars foremost, then Rahu/Saturn — occupies or aspects the 7th **with no benefic in the 7th**) OR the karaka is jammed between/with malefics → predict **divorced/separated**. *Override to stable if a benefic (esp. Venus/Moon own, or Jupiter) shares the 7th.*
4. **Love vs arranged (only if married is predicted).** If karaka or 7th-lord is in the 5th, or 5th-lord is own/exalted in the 5th with a 5–7 link → **love**; else **arranged**.
5. **Else → married-stable.**

### Highest-signal rules

1. **Manglik + Mars/Rahu in the 7th with no benefic there ⇒ divorce/separation.** 796,800,815,819 all Manglik-Y with a malefic in the 7th and no benefic → all split; contrast 764/824/830 (Manglik-Y but a benefic co-tenants the 7th) → all stable. (Benefic-in-7th is the "cancellation" flag.)
2. **7/9 divorced/separated are Manglik-Y**; only 6/16 stable-married are. Manglik matters *after* marriage, not before it.
3. **7th lord in 6/8/12 or debilitated/combust + no benefic aspecting the 7th + afflicted karaka ⇒ unmarried.** Fits 761,763,765,767,811,825,752 (the natives' own REASONs say the same).
4. **Karaka in the 12th decides by dignity:** weak Venus in 12th → 829 (no marriage), 808/826 (widowed); Venus *own* in 12th → 762 married. Read dignity, not just house.
5. **Love vs arranged = strength of the 5th.** Jupiter/7th-lord/5th-lord own-or-exalted **in the 5th** ⇒ love (772,804,816); a dignified 7th-lord sitting in the 7th with a quiet 5th ⇒ arranged (755,803). For women specifically, **Jupiter in the 5th = love marriage** (4/4 clean).

### Caveats

- **Manglik base rate is 52%** in this file, so it is only useful *conditioned* on a malefic actually loading the 7th and on the absence of a benefic there — never as a standalone.
- **Widowhood (5 cases) is essentially unpredicted (2/5).** These charts often have a *strong* karaka; spouse-longevity needs the 8th-from-7th (2nd house / maraka to the 7th) and dasha timing, which weren't decisive in the six requested factors.
- **Cells are tiny for the love/arranged split (2+2, plus 816).** The "Jupiter-in-5th ⇒ love" rule is clean here but rests on 5 charts.
- Several REASON lines invoke **navamsha (D9), chalit, Upapada/Darakaraka, nakshatra lords, and dasha timing** (795,801,808,829) that the tokenized D1 data can't reproduce — these are the residual misses (e.g. 795's neech-bhanga, 801's D9 Venus with Sat+Rahu → live-in).
- One cohort effect (chart 765: "most born 1981–1989, esp. 1988, are unmarried") is untestable without birth years and was ignored.
