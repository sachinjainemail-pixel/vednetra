# FOREIGN / RESIDENCE

## FOREIGN/RESIDENCE
n = 15 natives. Three outcome buckets (with [755]'s dual answer counted as home):

| Bucket | Natives | Count |
|---|---|---|
| **Home** (birth-city / another city of own state / own region) | 755, 771, 804, 829, 830 | 5 |
| **Other-state within country** | 752, 757, 764, 816 | 4 |
| **Abroad** (foreign country) | 763, 773, 792, 802, 803, 819 | 6 |

### Diagnostic factors (ranked)

1. **Planet occupying the 12th house (Rahu, Lagna-lord, or an exalted benefic)** — strongest ABROAD flag. Every native with a tenant in the 12th who was *not* pre-empted by a home rule went abroad: [763] Rahu in 12th (Cancer), [802] exalted Jupiter + Mercury in 12th (Cancer), [819] Lagna-lord Sun in 12th (Cancer). 3/3 abroad. (The only other 12th-tenant chart, [829] Venus+Ketu in 12th, stayed home — but it is caught first by the exalted-Lagna-lord rule below.) 12th = house of foreign lands / leaving the homeland; occupancy activates it.

2. **Exalted Lagna-lord sitting in a kendra (1/4/7/10)** — clean HOME flag. [804] Mercury exalted in 4th and [829] Mercury exalted in 1st both stayed rooted. 2/2. A strong, well-placed self-significator keeps the native anchored to the birth region. (Note: *own*-sign Lagna-lord is NOT enough — [792] Mars own in 1st went abroad.)

3. **Rahu in 9th / 11th / 12th (distance & foreign-gain houses)** — ABROAD. [773] Rahu 9th, [792] Rahu 11th, [763] Rahu 12th. Rahu = the great "foreign / mleccha" karaka; placed in the houses of long journeys (9), large gains (11) or exile (12) it pulls the native overseas.

4. **Rahu–Ketu on the 4th/10th axis (Ketu in 4th, or Rahu in 4th/10th)** — DISPLACEMENT from the home city. Present in [752] Rahu10/Ketu4, [816] Rahu10/Ketu4 (both other-state) and [803] Rahu10/Ketu4 (abroad). The 4th (home/roots) is hit, so the native leaves the birthplace — direction (state vs. abroad) is then decided by the 12th-lord's condition (see factor 5).

5. **Condition of the 12th lord** — separates abroad from mere other-state on the 4/10-axis charts. A **debilitated / neecha-bhanga 12th lord** channels the displacement overseas: [803] 12th-lord Mercury debilitated (neecha-bhanga) in 6th → abroad. A 12th lord merely parked in a domestic house (Venus in 4th [752], Sun in 2nd [816]) keeps it in-country → other-state.

6. **9th house occupied by a planet** — mild OTHER-STATE marker (medium-distance travel within the country): [757] Sun+Moon+Mercury in 9th, [764] Saturn in 9th. (Weak: [771] has Moon-exalted+Mars in 9th yet stayed in own state — the single exception.)

7. **Moon (mind/travel) and 7th/9th lord** — supporting, not decisive here. Moon in the 9th (long-journey mindset) co-occurs with three movers ([752],[757] other-state; [771] home) but does not cleanly separate the buckets; treated as tie-breaker only.

### Decision procedure
Apply in order; stop at the first match.

1. **HOME** — if the Lagna-lord is **exalted** and sits in a kendra (1/4/7/10). → [804],[829]
2. **ABROAD** — if any planet (Rahu, Lagna-lord, or an exalted benefic) **occupies the 12th house**. → [763],[802],[819]
3. **ABROAD** — if **Rahu is in the 9th, 11th, or 12th** (and the 12th is empty). → [773],[792]
4. **ABROAD** — if **Rahu/Ketu fall on the 4th–10th axis AND the 12th lord is debilitated / neecha-bhanga**. → [803]
5. **OTHER-STATE** — if the **9th house is occupied**, OR **Ketu is in the 4th / Rahu is in the 4th or 10th**. → [752],[757],[764],[816]
6. **HOME** — otherwise. → [755],[771](miss),[830]

### Back-test result
**14/15 exact (93%)**; 15/15 within one adjacent bucket.

Miss list:
- **[771]** — predicted OTHER-STATE (9th house occupied by exalted Moon + Mars), actual HOME (another city of own state). The three exaltations (Moon-9, Jupiter-11, Venus-7) mark an unusually "settled/contented" chart that overrode the 9th-house displacement signal — a settledness factor the procedure does not yet encode. This is a home-vs-other-state adjacent miss, not a home-vs-abroad error.

### Highest-signal rules
1. **A tenant in the 12th house → abroad.** 3/3 ([763] Rahu-12, [802] exa-Jupiter-12, [819] Lagna-lord Sun-12); the 4th such chart ([829]) is caught earlier by the exalted-Lagna-lord home rule.
2. **Exalted Lagna-lord in a kendra → stays home.** 2/2 ([804] Me-exa-H4, [829] Me-exa-H1). Own-sign is insufficient ([792] Mars-own-H1 went abroad).
3. **Rahu in 9/11/12 → abroad.** 3/3 counting [763] ([773] Rahu-9, [792] Rahu-11, [763] Rahu-12).
4. **Rahu/Ketu on the 4/10 axis → left the birthplace.** 3/3 leave ([752],[816] other-state; [803] abroad); the 12th-lord's dignity then sets the distance.
5. **9th occupied or Ketu-in-4th, with no 12th tenant → another state.** 4/4 on the un-pre-empted charts ([752],[757],[764],[816]).

### Caveats
- **Small n and overfit risk.** With only 6 abroad / 4 other-state / 5 home, rules 3–4 rest on 2–3 charts each; step 4 in particular is tuned to a single native ([803]). Expect the exact rate to fall below 93% out-of-sample.
- **Abroad vs. other-state is the fragile boundary.** Both share Rahu-in-kendra and 4/10-axis signals; only the 12th-lord's dignity separated them here, on thin evidence.
- **Residence is a state, not a dated event, so the dasha test is weak.** Still, posters' REASON lines consistently invoke a **Rahu, Ketu, or 12th-significator Mahadasha** as the *activator* of the move ([752] Ketu-MD with Ketu in 4th; [763] Rahu-MD with Rahu in 12th; [773] Jupiter-MD with NBRY toward the 12th; [803] Rahu). So while placement decides *whether/where*, a node/12th-lord dasha tends to decide *when*.
- **Moon and the 7th lord** added little discriminating power on this sample and were used only as tie-breakers.
